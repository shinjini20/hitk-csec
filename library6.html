Library Rules


  • 1. Library membership card is compulsory while issuing books for home reading. It is not transferable.
  • 2. The books must NOT be shelved by the users and should be left on the table.
  • 3. Books once issued will not be allowed to keep inside the library.
  • 4. Guidelines for photocopying should be followed.
  • 5. Students are not allowed in the library without membership cards
  • 6. Strict silence should be maintained inside the library.
  • 7. Carrying of bags, folders, personal books or any printed materials, laptop, walkman, food articles are strictly prohibited in the library. Mobile phones must be switched off before entering the library.
  • 8. Any damage or loss of book should be replaced within a week by a new one with prior intimation to the Librarian in advance.
  • 9. No project/ laboratory works, group discussions or any kind of class meetings would be allowed inside the library.
  • 10. Issued books along with the gate pass must be shown to the security personnel at the entrance before leaving the library.
  • 11. Loss of library/Identity card must be reported to the Librarian immediately. For a duplicate card, all supporting documents regarding its loss should be submitted to the Administration
  • 12. For any help, approach “Information Desk” or any library staff.
